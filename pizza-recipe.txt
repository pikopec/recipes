Ingredients:
 
 Dough
 
 - 1kg flour
 - 50g yeast
 - 1 pinch of salt
 - 1 spoon of olive oil
 - 2 cups of water
 
 Topping
 
 - 600g chesse
 - 400g ham
 - 4 spoons of tomato concentrate
 - olives
 - 4 champignons
 
 
Directions:
 
 1. Mix dough ingredients in a bowl until smooth.
 2. Preheat oven to 180 degrees C.
 3. Split dough in two and form flat circles.
 4. Put tomato concentrate evenly on circles, cover it with ham strips 
	and cheese.
 5. Bake 40 minutes.
